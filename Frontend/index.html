Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really anonymous?

Yes, absolutely. We don't require any personal information to use our chat service. Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are encrypted and we don't store any personally identifiable information.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No, VirtualFriend is not a replacement for professional therapy. It's a tool for emotional support and can complement professional help. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed mental health professional.

What kind of issues can I discuss?

You can talk about anything that's on your mind - school stress, relationships, family issues, self-esteem, or anything else you're going through. Your virtual friend is here to listen and provide support.

How does the AI understand my feelings?

Our AI has been specially trained to recognize emotional cues and respond with empathy and understanding. It uses natural language processing to understand your messages and provide supportive responses tailored to your emotional state.
